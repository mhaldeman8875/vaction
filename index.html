Welcome to our Vaction site

So far we've spent nearly a week in Argentina and have had many adventures. Here are our stories so far:

Day 1: Feral Dogs

As soon as we left the airport we found ourselves surrounded by a pack of feral dogs and had to sacrifice most of our food in order to escape them to our hostel.

Day 2: Death Squads

Today, we accidentally wandered into a riot in the heart of Buenos Aires and almost got caught in the middle of an exchange of fire between a local milita and the federales. The militia was quickly overwhelmed and wiped out by what must be called a "death squad" at this point, given their military expertise.

Day 4: Montezuma's Revenge

We were drunk and throwing bottles. One of the bottles hit the Montezuma family celebrating a birthday next door. In retaliation the forced laxatives down our throats. They were avenged.

Day 7: We done fucked up

We took refuge from the Montezuma family by retreating into the mountains. Unfortunately we got lost and wound up wandering into a neighboring country. After days without any food and worsening dysentary we located a road and followed it to a town. We were quickly encarcerated until we were ruled out as illegal aliens.
